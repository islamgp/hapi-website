# HAPI Brand Guide

## 1. Brand essence

HAPI is clean, practical, intelligent, and human.

The brand should feel like a premium modern wellness company — not a pharmacy, not a gym brand, not a sugary drink brand.

## 2. Brand tagline options

Primary:

> Real ingredients. Real impact.

Additional options:

- Designed for real life.
- Functional drinks for modern days.
- Smart energy. Calm focus. Real recovery.
- Daily support, made simple.

## 3. Visual personality

The HAPI visual world should combine:

- nature;
- clean science;
- premium minimalism;
- soft lifestyle photography;
- calm product presentation;
- clear functional categories.

## 4. Color direction

Suggested palette:

- Deep forest / dark green for trust and premium nature.
- Soft cream / warm white for cleanliness.
- Sage green for wellness.
- Soft orange for morning energy.
- Lavender for reset and calm.
- Fresh blue for travel and hydration.
- Warm beige for smart meal.

## 5. Typography direction

Use modern sans-serif typography.

The design should feel:

- minimal;
- premium;
- readable;
- not childish;
- not overly medical.

## 6. Imagery

Preferred imagery:

- clean product renders;
- natural ingredients;
- real people in daily situations;
- mothers and parents;
- travelers;
- professionals;
- athletes;
- calm home scenes.

Avoid:

- exaggerated medical visuals;
- fake laboratory clichés;
- over-muscular fitness imagery;
- cheap stock photos;
- chaotic backgrounds.

## 7. Voice

HAPI speaks simply:

Bad:
> Revolutionary neuro-metabolic performance drink.

Good:
> Clean focus for the part of the day that needs your best attention.

## 8. Claim discipline

All copy must avoid disease claims.

Use:
- supports;
- helps maintain;
- designed for;
- formulated with;
- made for moments when.

Avoid:
- treats;
- cures;
- prevents disease;
- guarantees;
- medically proven unless properly documented.
