# HAPI Manifesto

We are not creating drinks.
We are creating better human states throughout the day.

## Why HAPI Exists

Modern people live faster than ever.

They wake up tired.
They work in constant noise.
They eat on the go.
They travel.
They raise children.
They train.
They face stress.

The world usually offers two paths:

stimulate the body harder,
or treat the consequences later.

HAPI chooses a third path:

help people stay in their best possible state every day.
