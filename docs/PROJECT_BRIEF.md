# HAPI Project Brief

## 1. What is HAPI?

HAPI is a functional drinks and smart meals brand designed for real moments of modern life: recovery, focus, morning energy, calm reset, travel, parenting, work, and daily nutrition.

HAPI is not a medical brand. It is not a miracle supplement brand. It is a practical, modern wellness brand built around clean formulas, clear use cases, and honest communication.

## 2. Brand promise

Functional products that help people feel ready for the next part of their day.

## 3. Core positioning

HAPI sits at the intersection of:

- functional beverages;
- smart nutrition;
- daily wellness;
- science-backed ingredients;
- modern lifestyle;
- practical convenience.

## 4. Main product lines

### Functional drinks

- HAPI RECOVERY
- HAPI MORNING
- HAPI RESET
- HAPI FOCUS
- HAPI TRAVEL DAY
- HAPI TRAVEL NIGHT

### Smart meals

- HAPI SMART MEAL
- HAPI MOM
- HAPI DAILY
- HAPI TRAVEL BOX

## 5. Target users

- busy professionals;
- young parents;
- breastfeeding mothers;
- athletes and fitness users;
- travelers;
- drivers;
- students;
- people with irregular schedules.

## 6. What HAPI solves

HAPI helps users choose a product by situation:

- I need energy.
- I need focus.
- I need recovery.
- I need calm.
- I am traveling.
- I need a quick smart meal.
- I am taking care of a baby and need something practical.

## 7. What HAPI must never do

HAPI must not:

- make unsupported medical claims;
- promise treatment or cure;
- sound like a cheap supplement brand;
- overuse aggressive fitness marketing;
- use fake science;
- confuse users with complicated wording;
- look like a generic beverage brand.

## 8. Website objective

The website must introduce HAPI as a premium functional brand and serve four audiences:

1. customers;
2. retail and B2B partners;
3. investors;
4. future distributors.

## 9. Tone

Clear, confident, calm, modern, human, science-aware.

HAPI should sound intelligent but not cold.
