This document is the first source of truth for HAPI products. Website data should later be moved into structured code files such as `data/products.ts`.

## HAPI RECOVERY

### Use case

After heat, sweating, sports, travel, long workdays, or dehydration risk.

### Positioning

Hydration and recovery support.

### Draft ingredients

- Sodium
- Potassium
- Magnesium citrate
- Chloride
- Citrate
- Vitamin C
- B vitamins
- Coconut water
- Lime flavor

### Short copy

Replenish. Restore. Feel your best.

### Claim discipline

Do not claim treatment of dehydration disease, hangover cure, or medical electrolyte therapy.
